Hamburger

Description

A classic hamburger with juicy beef patty, fresh vegetables, and melted cheese served in a soft bun.

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Burger bun
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Season the beef patty with salt and pepper
  2. Grill the patty for 4 minutes each side
  3. Toast the burger bun
  4. Place lettuce and tomato on the bottom bun
  5. Add the patty and cheese on top
  6. Add onion, ketchup and mustard
  7. Close with the top bun and serve
