Sel Roti

Description

A traditional Nepali ring-shaped fried bread made from rice flour, crispy outside and soft inside, commonly made during festivals like Dashain and Tihar.

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Milk
  • Water

Steps

  1. Soak rice overnight and grind into smooth batter
  2. Add sugar, ghee, milk and cardamom to the batter
  3. Mix well until smooth and thick consistency
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan
  5. Pour batter in circular motion to form ring shape
  6. Fry until golden brown on both sides
  7. Remove and drain excess oil
  8. Serve warm with yogurt or achar
