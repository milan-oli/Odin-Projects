Mo:Mo

Description

Steamed Nepali dumplings filled with spiced minced meat, served with achar.

Ingredients

  • Minced chicken or buff
  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Coriander
  • Cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil
  • Tomato achar

Steps

  1. Mix minced meat with chopped onion, garlic, ginger and spices
  2. Take a dumpling wrapper and place filling in the center
  3. Fold and seal the edges tightly
  4. Place momos in a greased steamer
  5. Steam for 15 minutes
  6. Serve hot with tomato achar
